About Me

I'm a seasoned marketing executive with a passion for transforming business growth through strategic marketing leadership.

With over 15 years of experience across diverse industries, I specialize in bringing Fortune 500 marketing strategies to growth-stage companies. My approach combines data-driven decision making with creative storytelling to build memorable brands that resonate with target audiences.

Throughout my career, I've helped businesses navigate digital transformation, build high-performance marketing teams, and implement integrated marketing strategies that drive measurable business results.

As a Fractional CMO, I partner with organizations looking for senior marketing leadership without the full-time commitment or overhead. This arrangement provides my clients with strategic direction, marketing expertise, and hands-on support exactly when needed.

My mission is to help ambitious businesses unlock their growth potential through marketing strategies that are both innovative and sustainable. I believe in creating marketing systems that continue delivering results long after our engagement ends.

In addition to my client work, I regularly contribute to industry publications and speak at marketing conferences on topics ranging from marketing leadership to digital transformation.
